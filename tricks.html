Tricks

The Strong Sleeper

To do a strong sleeper you need to throw the yo-yo down (really hard) and keep it still spinning

Walking the dog

Walking the dog is a trick where you let the yo-yo sleep (which is where you let the yo-yo stay in the same place in this case it would stay at the bottom still spinning) and then you put the yo-yo on the ground. Then let it roll on the ground.

The Elevator

The yo-yo trick makes your yo-yo look like it is going up like a elevator. What you need to do for this trick is you first throw the yo-yo and then spread your hands apart.

The Forward Pass

This yo-yo trick looks like you're throwing the yo-yo forward. How you do this trick is, you first put your palm facing dowm. Then you put your arm to the side. After that you throw the yo-yo out in front of you. Lasty, you flip your palm facing up (When the string is all the way out) which will make the yo-yo come back to you.

Around The World

To do an around the world you first need to do a forward pass, and just keep the yo-yo sleeping when you throw it around your self.

The Creeper

First, you need to let the yo-yo into a strong sleeper. Then rock the yo-yo. Lastly, let the yo-yo go on the ground way in front of you and then tug, and it will roll in front of you.
