Yo-Yos

Yoyoing is a hobie that involves a string and a yo-yo. There is a bunch of tricks you can do with it, like walking the dog. There are also competitions for yoyoing like The World Yo-Yo Contest.

Top 5 Yo-Yo Players

  1. Johnnie DelValle (From America)
  2. Jensen Kimmitt (From Canada)
  3. Tomonari ‘Black’ Ishiguro (From Japan)
  4. Yuuki Spencer (From America)
  5. Hiroyuki Suzuki (From Japan)

The World Yo-Yo Contest is a yoyo competition that is a worldwide competitive district. It is also called the most prestigious yo-yo competition in the world. The winner of the competition in any of the six championship divisions is deemed the World Yo-Yo Champion.
